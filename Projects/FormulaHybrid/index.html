Formula Hybred

The Formula Hybrid Competition takes place during the spring of every ye. It is regarded as the most complex and dynamic of the series of competitions hosted by the Society of Automotive Engineers for Collegiate Design. THe purpose of the competittion is to create the most efficient formula-style electric or plug-in hybrid racecar. Each car competes in numerous events to evaluate performance and fuelefficiency. During the event a special emphasis has ben put on innovating new ways and adapting new techniques to increase the performance of the vehicle.

As a part of the McMaster Formula Hybrid team I was able to work on the software and in turn help improve the performance of the car.I was responsible for working on the algorhythem to deteck and correct for the wheel slipping during acceleration and breaking events of the vehicle. I found this an excillent way to apply my knowledge of filters and real time systems from class. Filters were use to reduce noise in the system and help compensate for the sampling frequency that was limitted by the speed of the CAN network of the vehicle. Unfortunately time constraints didn't allow for the proper tesing of the software and a decision was made to not include the code during the competition to ensure the driver's safety.
