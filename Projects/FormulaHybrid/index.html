Formula Hybred

The formula hybrid competition takes place during the spring of every ye. It is regarded as the most complex and dynamic of the series of competitions hosted by the Society of Automotive Engineers for Collegiate Design. THe purpose of the competittion is to create the most efficient formula-style electric or plug-in hybrid racecar. Each car competes in numerous events to evaluate performance and fuelefficiency. During the event a special emphasis has ben put on innovating new ways and adapting new techniques to increase the performance of the vehicle.

This project has many different roles as each team is required to design, simulate, modify and test the vehicle powertrains. For the past year I have been involved with the low level software development of the car. I have helped program new functionality and in the process learned a lot about the development process of the car. During the year i have been able to apply my knowledge in C# to programming freescale processors and inturn execute cerain tasks. furthermore I was able to apply my knowledge about the software development process and create appropriate documentation before constructing efficient code.
