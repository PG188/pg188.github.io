Reflection

My co-op experience at General Dynamics proved to be both intriguing and enlightening. It marked the furthest distance I had traveled from home for work, and the entire experience, from my living arrangements to the workplace culture, was notably distinct from my previous experiences. I had the privilege of working within a highly motivated, youthful team tasked with the maintenance of critical code running on one of Canada's surveillance aircraft. Working in a company that deals with controlled goods, I gained valuable insights into the stringent security protocols required to safeguard such sensitive materials. Additionally, a strong emphasis was placed on meticulous documentation, as many of the changes implemented would have far-reaching impacts on the product for decades to come.
