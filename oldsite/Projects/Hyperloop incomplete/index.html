Hyperloop Competition

The Hyperloop comptition organised by Space X with an aim to help accelerate the development of the Hyperloop protoype. Hosted at Texas A&M University, the event invited 120 schools to put forward thier innovative ideas and designs to the test. Each team's plan was judged by a pannel of highly qualified engineers and employees from Space X and Tesla.

My primary role on the team was to design and render the team's proposed Hyperloop Pod. A part of my job was to ensure that all the parts and subsystems has suffecient space. Furthermore I had to ensire that none of the moving components would interfear with each other and what safety measures would be in place to prevent it. The most dificult challenge was to add all the features the team wanted in the dimensions specified provided by the organisers while ensuring theere was suffecient space for the passengers to be confortably seated. Part of my job involved analysing conponents to determine how they would perform with various amounts of stress and strain. This was done using the stress analysis tools provided in Autodesk Inventor.
