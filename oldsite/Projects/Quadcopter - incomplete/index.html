Quadcopter Build

In my spare time I have been working on a few quadcopter CADs. My aim was to have an easy to build quad that was modular and able to support a few cuztom built attachments. This would allow be to have a quick and dirty base to practice programing a quad and attempt to have it perform a few complex tasks. The design was meant to be cheap and quick to manufacute with the assistance of a 3D Printer. The overall build budget for the basic sensorless quad is approximately $100.
